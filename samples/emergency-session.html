The inside of Harry's skull smells like a cold ashtray and — humiliatingly — someone else's cigarettes. Kim's brand. Nobody requested that detail. It is entered into the minutes anyway.

Someone is banging a gavel that turns out, on closer inspection, to be a human molar.

ANCIENT REPTILIAN BRAIN — we were sleeping. we do not convene. we secrete. who has woken the chamber.

THE LIMBIC SYSTEM (already crying) — I was having the nice dream. The one with the boat. Put me back in the boat.

The session is called to order anyway. A single item on the agenda, read aloud off a damp index card in twenty-four-part harmony:

MOTION ON THE FLOOR: Are we in love with Kim Kitsuragi?

The Thought Cabinet was tasked with processing this three nights ago. It has returned its verdict. The verdict is a shrug the size of a cathedral.

INCONCLUSIVE.

The Parliament loses its entire mind.

THE INTELLECT DELEGATION takes the floor

LOGIC [Medium: Failure] — If we cross-reference the prolonged eye contact against the documented cigarette transfers, the only sound conclusion is a mild iron deficiency. Case closed.

ENCYCLOPEDIA [Trivial: Success] — Fun fact: in Old Oranjese the word for partner shares a root with load-bearing wall. Nobody asked. Filing it anyway.

RHETORIC [Challenging: Success] — I can win this. Give me eleven minutes, a clean shirt, and his undivided attention and I will argue the man into reciprocating.

DRAMA [Formidable: Critical Failure] — O, doomed prince of Precinct 41! He lent us his scarf, my lords, and we WORE it, and we have not WASHED it—

CONCEPTUALIZATION [Easy: Success] — His rolled-up cuff is a study in restraint. Negative space. The whole forearm is a fresco. Someone should commission it.

VISUAL CALCULUS [Medium: Success] — Distance across the Kineema gearshift: thirty-one centimetres. Closing it would take zero-point-eight seconds. I have run the trajectory four hundred times. For the Law.

THE PSYCHE DELEGATION demands to be heard

VOLITION [Medium: Success] — Order. Order. We are not going to spiral about this at — checks — four in the morning. We will discuss it like adults and then never again.

INLAND EMPIRE [Heroic: Success] — The radiator clicked twice when he almost smiled. That was a sign. The world is telling us something and the something is sacred.

EMPATHY [Formidable: Failure] — He sighed at us over the body this morning. He's tired of us. He's going to request a transfer and a quieter, smaller detective.

AUTHORITY [Challenging: Critical Failure] — I OUTRANK HIM. I could simply order him to lo— no. No. That's a war crime. Strike it from the record.

ESPRIT DE CORPS [Easy: Success] — Meanwhile, somewhere far away from here, Jean Vicquemare sits bolt upright for no reason he can name.

SUGGESTION [Medium: Failure] — What if we said: Detective, your closure rate isn't the only thing in this room that's killer. ...I'm still workshopping it. Don't look at me like that.

THE PHYSIQUE DELEGATION will not be silenced

ENDURANCE [Trivial: Success] — We have been awake inside our own skull for four hours and the body would simply like everyone to know that it aches.

PAIN THRESHOLD [Challenging: Success] — Feelings can't hurt us if we just absorb them like a punch. ...That's the twelfth punch. We are completely fine. We are thriving.

PHYSICAL INSTRUMENT [Easy: Success] — He's so small, though. We could fold him into a coat pocket. For safety. His. Mostly his.

ELECTROCHEMISTRY [Trivial: Critical Success] — Oh, this is easy. We want to climb him like a fire escape. MOTION TO ADJOURN TO HIS BUNK. Seconded? Somebody second me.

SHIVERS [Legendary: Success] — Rain on Martinaise. A tram brakes two streets over. Somewhere in a rented room Kim Kitsuragi is asleep and breathing slow, and the city is counting his breaths so you don't have to.

HALF-LIGHT [Formidable: Failure] — NO. ABORT. If we say it OUT LOUD he LEAVES and then we DIE alone in a ditch that smells faintly of orange pekoe—

THE MOTORICS DELEGATION, mostly fidgeting

HAND/EYE COORDINATION [Medium: Failure] — I dropped the metaphorical lighter. Also the real one. He picked up both and handed them back without looking. That, gentlemen, is the whole problem.

PERCEPTION [Heroic: Success] — He smells like gun oil and orange pekoe and the warm back of his neck when he leans down over the case file and his glasses slide and he pushes them up with one knuckle and—

REACTION SPEED [Challenging: Failure] — When he smiled we said "cool" four full seconds too late. And also, somehow, slightly before.

SAVOIR FAIRE [Formidable: Critical Failure] — Watch me exit this debate with absolute panache — (walks directly into the ventricle wall)

INTERFACING [Easy: Success] — I just want to hand him things. Gearshifts. Lighters. The case file. The keys. My en— a pen. A pen.

COMPOSURE [Challenging: Failure] — Gentlemen. Our hands are shaking. Put the molar down. ...He let one corner of his mouth move yesterday. For us. I logged the time.

The motion is put to a vote.

Twelve aye. Twelve nay. The Ancient Reptilian Brain abstains out of pure spite. The Limbic System votes, tearfully, "boat."

Inconclusive remains inconclusive.

ESPRIT DE CORPS [Easy: Success] — Across the river, Jean exhales smoke into the dark and mutters, "He's doing something stupid right now. I can feel it." He is correct. He files it under Tuesday and goes back to sleep.

The molar comes down one final time. Session adjourned. Nothing decided. Nothing, in this chamber, is ever decided.

Harry wakes at 7:20 — ten minutes before the vow — mouth like a carpet, heart doing something it flatly refuses to name. On the nightstand: a fresh, unopened pack of the Astras. Kim's brand. He doesn't remember buying them. He taps one out, doesn't light it, just holds it between two fingers, and goes to find the kettle.
